🃏 How to Play the Memory Game 🃏

Goal: Find all the matching pairs of cards with the fewest moves and in the shortest time!


🔹Game Instructions:

  1. Start the Game: Begin the game by clicking any card to flip it over and reveal the image.
  2. Flip Cards: Click a second card to try and find its matching pair.
  3. Match or Mismatch: If the two cards match, they will stay face up. If they don’t match, they will flip back.
  4. Keep Going: Continue flipping cards and finding pairs until all cards are matched.
  5. Win the Game: The game ends when all pairs are found or when the timer ends, which ever one happens first! Its a race against the timer to find all the matches -- can you find them all in time?

💡 Tips for Success:

  1. Focus on memorising card positions after each flip.
  2. Be quicker than the timer!

🎉 Congratulations! You won the game! 🎉
